About Us

Quality, Reliability and Cost-Savings are the foundations of Nadic Engineering Services, Inc. Nadic's highly qualified and experienced professional, technical and administrative staff work together to accomplish your needs. Accuracy and on-time submittal are our commitment to every project, whether it is a single residential lot, a large commercial/office development, or a complex transportation project.

As a small, growing firm, Nadic encourages the staff to work closely together, challenging each other, learning from each other and developing the team concept. We are always looking for individuals who have a vision for the future and are willing to learn and develop their skills. The services we provide our clients demand excellence in our work efforts and attention to the client. Nadic provides opportunities for every staff to contribute to the team.

We are keenly aware that the commitment and qualifications of our staff are our most valuable asset and they will influence the future of our firm. Therefore, we are dedicated to making sure that each member of our staff brings a new and complementary dimension to our team and contributes positively to our growth and meeting the needs of our clients. We are totally committed to personal and corporate integrity, professional and personal ethics, open communication, equal opportunity, personal and career development at all levels in the company, and a team approach that values the differences in individual members. Nadic is committed to create quality and positive atmosphere conducive to develop the individual potentials and skills. We believe in developing the individualís physical, intellectual and emotional growth as well as dignity in the engineering environment. Our belief is that our team must share our values and work ethic, thrive in this environment, and allow our organization to thrive as well.

We believe in working and playing together with a spirit of fun and cooperation that is often not found in larger, more fragmented firms. We are proud of our commitment to support our employeesí growth and advancement.

Nadic offers competitive salaries and generous benefits including:

  • Medical
  • Dental
  • 401 (k) program
  • Profit-sharing
  • Professional training and educational assistance
  • Licensure assistance
  • Bonus program
  • Family leave
  • Paid personal time off

We are committed to providing benefits that allow each member of staff to achieve a healthy balance of the professional aspects of their life. We are focused to having happy and healthy employees who can achieve more in mind, body and spirit!
